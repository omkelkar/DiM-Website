Our History

Gourmet Bistro opened its doors in 1990 with a mission to bring high-quality, delicious food to our community. Founded by Chef John Doe, our restaurant has grown from a small family-run establishment to a well-loved dining destination.

Our Mission

At Gourmet Bistro, our mission is to provide an exceptional dining experience by serving high-quality, innovative dishes made with the freshest ingredients. We strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels at home.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the best ingredients to create our dishes.
  • Innovation: We continuously experiment with new recipes and techniques.
  • Customer Service: We are committed to providing excellent service to every guest.
  • Community: We support local farmers and businesses.

Our Team

Chef John Doe

Our head chef and founder, John has over 30 years of culinary experience. He trained at the prestigious Culinary Institute of America and has worked in top restaurants around the world. John is passionate about creating innovative dishes that delight and inspire.

Manager Jane Smith

Jane ensures that every guest has a wonderful dining experience. With a background in hospitality management and a keen eye for detail, Jane leads our front-of-house team with warmth and professionalism.

Sous Chef Emma Brown

Emma is our talented sous chef, assisting John in the kitchen and bringing her own creative flair to the menu. She specializes in modern cuisine and has a knack for combining unexpected flavors in delicious ways.

Pastry Chef Michael Lee

Michael is our master pastry chef, known for his exquisite desserts that are as beautiful as they are tasty. With a degree in patisserie and confectionery arts, Michael adds a sweet touch to our dining experience.
